## INTRODUCTION

**Green County** is a *fantasy* map based on an actual **10.2x10.2 km** area near [Allensville - Ohio](https://goo.gl/maps/XVVFk1oYRXkTHcZL6). As a fantasy map some aspects are close to reality (*North American aspect as possible with some custom assets*), but some liberties have been taken for gameplay reasons.

The roadmap of this project is available on Trello : [https://trello.com/b/WnXjljlt/roadmap-greencounty](https://trello.com/b/WnXjljlt/roadmap-greencounty).

## BACKGROUND / LORE

> The year is 2023, shortly after the COVID-19 pandemic a new unknown and seemingly incurable disease has hit the North American continent. The inhabitants of the regions at risk have been evacuated in emergency at west by the national guard a few months ago, however there are rumors of survivors in the confined areas and strange behavior has been observed. Despite signs of hasty evacuations, Green County seems to have regained a peaceful and quiet side... will you be strong enough to see for yourself?

## GAMEPLAY INFORMATIONS

Unlike Chernarus and Livonia where the player's direction of deplacement is South to North and North to South, **Green County has an West to East progression**. Two "*militaries*" zones are located in the south and in the north, however the policy is spreading out the military loot and to avoid large areas such as Tisy for example. The county is sparsely populated, especially in the north where forests predominate. The central area of the county is very agriculturally oriented, therefore *public services are weak* there, the only hospitals,firestation and police station in the area are in **Leaf Town** the main town of the county, that is balanced by some dynamics spawns (*Police cars, Firetrucks and Ambulances*). 
A central river runs laterally through the map much like the Livonia River. Numerous lakes and ponds are present especially in the east section. Some waterstreams can be discovered by walking through the forests.

*This map will be perfect for medium sized servers (20/40 players) who appreciate forest spaces and extensive road/hiking networks for PVE, and/or PVP server who player like a big town for intensive fights and large flelds for sniping.*
